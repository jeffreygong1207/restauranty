Product overview:
ResoFlow is a restaurant-controlled no-show prevention and reservation recovery platform.

Core pitch:
ResoFlow helps restaurants detect at-risk reservations before they become no-shows, contact diners, enforce restaurant-defined recovery policies, match verified replacement diners, prevent abuse, and recover tables before revenue is lost.

Important positioning:
- ResoFlow is NOT a reservation scalping marketplace.
- ResoFlow is NOT a bot that grabs restaurant tables.
- ResoFlow is NOT unauthorized resale.
- ResoFlow is NOT a black-market reservation exchange.
- Restaurants remain in control.
- Paid resale is disabled by default.
- Restaurant approval is required by default.
- Human verification is required for replacement diners by default.
- Recovery flows are policy-governed and audit-logged.
- The product is about responsible cancellation, verified waitlist refill, and restaurant-approved recovery.

RiskAgent:
Implement deterministic scoring. Do not rely on an LLM for business decisions.

Inputs:
- reservation
- diner
- restaurant
- policy
- current time
- historical behavior
- confirmation status
- reminder state
- party size
- high-demand slot
- card on file
- booking age

Formula:
Base: 10

Add:
- unconfirmed within confirmation window: +20
- final confirmation ignored: +24
- reminder not opened: +14
- prior late cancellation: +12 each, max +30
- prior no-show: +20 each, max +40
- reservation within 4 hours and not confirmed: +12
- high-demand slot: +8
- party size >= 5: +10
- no card on file: +8
- diner trust score < 50: +15
- multiple active reservations same night: +12

Subtract:
- verified human: -8
- card on file: -6
- trust score >= 85: -10
- confirmed: -30
- completedReservations >= 5 and no no-shows: -8

Clamp to 0–100.

Risk levels:
0–29 low
30–59 medium
60–79 high
80–100 critical

Return:
- score
- level
- weighted factors
- explanation
- recommended action

Recommended actions:
- score < 30: hold
- 30–59: monitor
- 60–79: send final confirmation
- 80–100: send final confirmation and prepare waitlist recovery
- released_by_diner: activate waitlist recovery
- suspicious transfer: require manager review
